WTF is This?!

PubConf is a comedy variety show for software folks. We partner with conferences around the world to bring you lighthearted fun and community in a non-professional setting.

Is this a joke?

Yes. Well, kind of. We're really throwing this party, these speakers are really going to speak on these topics, and we're really going to have some drinks. But it's all light-hearted and fun.

Is this a professional event?

No, This is a comedy variety show at a pub.

Can I go to PubConf if I didn't attend KCDC?

Absolutely! While PubConf is in partnership with KCDC, it is a separately ticketed event and we'd love to have you there!

What's included with my ticket?

Your ticket includes admission, pub-style buffet food (with plenty of veggie options), alcoholic and soft drinks, a fantastic show, awesome people - everything you need for a great night out.

And remember: PubConf is a one-of-a-kind live event. We won't be publishing photos or videos online, and you won't be able to watch it later on YouTube. The only way to experience PubConf is to get a ticket and see it for yourself.
